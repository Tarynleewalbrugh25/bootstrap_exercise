Why Trees Are Important!

Ecological & Environmental Value Trees contribute to their environment by providing oxygen, improving air quality, climate amelioration, conserving water, preserving soil, and supporting wildlife. During the process of photosynthesis, trees take in carbon dioxide and produce the oxygen we breathe.

What Are The Benefits Of Trees

Trees are one of the best partners when it comes to agriculture. They act positively in several ways: they reduce soil erosion, increase fertility and help soil obtain moisture. Fallen tree leaves lower reduce soil temperature and prevent soil from losing too much moisture.

Ways To keep Trees Healthy

  1. Prune Your Trees
  2. Water Your Trees
  3. Fertilize Your Trees
  4. Protect Your Trees from Pests
  5. Monitor Your Trees for Diseases
